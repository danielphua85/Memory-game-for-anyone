📜 How to Play 怎么玩
👀 Step 1 — Memorise (5 sec): Cards are revealed. Remember each card and its position!
🔄 Step 2 — Shuffle (30 sec): Cards flip over and physically swap places. Stay focused!
🤔 Step 3 — Guess: Choose the correct order from multiple choice. 选择正确顺序！
🎉 Correct = Awesome!  |  😅 Wrong = Better Luck Next Time!
